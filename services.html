Our Services
Transportation - Aqua Domita is capable of transporting large freight ranging from sizes that are small and could fit in a canter truck to machinery that requires 40-foot trailers.
Our partners and us ensure that a large variety of solutions are available to our customers such that no job is deemed too complicated.
Assembly - Going together with the lifting aspect, our team also specializes in the assembly and disassembly of small to medium sized industries and warehouses. Our trucks and cranes are able to maneuver to safely place and connect different components of an assembly as it would be from source to destination. Our team has the expertise to move large generators, boilers, motors, water tanks and engines into place using specialized equipment such as rollers, chain block systems, winches and straps. Precision and accuracy is our motto. Recovery - Aqua Domita provides recovery services for all types of vehicles. Whether it is a broken down vehicle, road traffic accident or a car in a ditch somewhere, out team has the expertise to safely extract and transport the vehicle to the destination of choice.
