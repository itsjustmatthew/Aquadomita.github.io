Aqua Domita was founded in 2004 by Mr. John Kago to tackle the large need for affordable logistics solutions in Kenya. Since it's start with one Bedford truck, the company has grown to a fleet of 20 foot and 40 foot self-loading trailers with various partnerships with other transportation companies. Aqua Domita has over 1500 jobs under its name both in the private and public sector. Notable clients include building contractors, Automotive manufacturers specializing in leasing and selling Generators, private individuals, Oil and water drilling companies, Agricultural manufacturers and industries among others. Clients can attest that the company is known for it's work ethic, adherence to safety standards, professionalism and high-quality machinery. Aqua Domita continues to push towards the future and dreams of establishing itself as the key player in logistics solutions in Kenya.
